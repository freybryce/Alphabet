Our Mission:

To give everyone the power to navigate all of their favorite social media sites in one place. There will never be a problem of having multiple tabs open for each social media account. NOW, there will be access to Reddit, Giphy, YouTube, and Instagram search on LIT.

About Us:

Our company, Alphabet, was created in Summer 2016, at the Seattle Google offices, with a goal to change the social media space as the world knows it. It was founded by Amy Yee, Bryce Frey, and Clay Clark hence the company name Alphabet (ABC)
